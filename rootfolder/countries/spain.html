Cuisines from Spain

Paella

Paella (pai·ei·uh) is a classic Spanish rice dish made with rice, saffron, vegetables, chicken, and seafood cooked and served in one pan.

One of Spain's most beloved tapas, tortilla española is a tender, lightly runny omelet filled with olive oil-poached potatoes.

Croquetas

A croquette is a type of dumpling consisting of a thick binder combined with a filling, which is breaded and deep-fried, and served as a side dish, a snack, or fast food worldwide. The binder is typically a thick béchamel or brown sauce, mashed potatoes, wheat flour or wheat bread.
