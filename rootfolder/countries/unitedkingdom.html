Full English Breakfast

The traditional full English breakfast, treated as a dish rather than a meal, includes bacon (traditionally back bacon), fried, poached or scrambled eggs, fried or grilled tomatoes, fried mushrooms, fried bread or buttered toast, and sausages. Black pudding, baked beans, and bubble and squeak are also often included. https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Full_breakfast

Sausages ("Bangers") and Mash

Bangers & Mash is a dish consisting of sausages served with mashed potatoes and onion gravy. Also known as sausages and mash, it’s a quintessential British dish that’s served throughout Great Britain, traditionally in pubs, and a survey a few years ago ranked it as Britain’s most popular comfort food.
